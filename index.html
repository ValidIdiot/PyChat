What Is PyChat?!?

PyChat is a free and safe program made specifically for chatting with friends!

Why is it called "PyChat?"

The reason for the name "PyChat" is because it was written entirely in Python using the tKinter, socket, and threading modules.

Why use PyChat when you can use Discord?

Don't. Only use PyChat for when you want to have a safe and private chat with your friend(s).
